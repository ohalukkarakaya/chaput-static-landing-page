What is Chaput?

A social conversation app built around profiles, permission and visibility

Chaput is a social app where conversations can become content. Instead of dropping people into random anonymous chats, Chaput helps users discover profiles, connect through approved follow relationships and start a conversation by leaving one Chaput on someone's profile.

If the recipient replies within 24 hours, the exchange becomes a conversation. Depending on the profile's privacy settings, those conversations can also become discoverable content for the appropriate audience.

Private first

New accounts are private by default. Chaput uses approved connections, follow requests and visibility rules to keep first contact intentional.

One thoughtful start

A sender can leave one first Chaput and cannot keep messaging the same person while waiting for a reply. The product is designed to reduce repeated unsolicited contact.

Conversations that can be discovered

Chaput turns appropriate conversations into readable profile content according to each profile's privacy settings, instead of keeping every exchange trapped in a private inbox.

Frequently asked questions

Questions users and search systems usually ask first

The FAQ below is visible, crawlable and matched by the JSON-LD on this page. It explains what Chaput is, how the messaging flow works and how privacy controls shape each conversation.

What is Chaput?

Chaput is a social app where people discover profiles, connect through approved follow relationships and start conversations with one intentional first message. Depending on each profile's privacy settings, a conversation can also become discoverable content that other permitted users can read.

How does Chaput work?

You discover a profile, request to follow when required and leave one Chaput after the connection is approved. If the recipient replies within 24 hours, the exchange becomes a conversation. Until a reply is received, the sender cannot continue sending messages to that person.

Is Chaput an anonymous random chat app?

No. Chaput does not place users into roulette-style anonymous chats. Interaction is based on persistent in-app profiles, recommended people, approved follow relationships and privacy settings rather than anonymous random matching.

Is Chaput a dating app?

Chaput is a social conversation app rather than a traditional dating or matchmaking service. People can meet and talk through shared interests, but the product does not randomly match users or promise romantic outcomes.

Can everyone read my conversations?

No. Conversation visibility depends on the relevant profile and privacy settings. Private profiles limit who can access their content, while the people participating in a conversation retain the access allowed by the product's visibility rules.

Can someone keep messaging me if I do not reply?

No. A sender can leave one initial Chaput and cannot continue sending messages to the same person while waiting for a reply. That limit is part of Chaput's approach to more intentional first contact.

What happens if a Chaput is not answered within 24 hours?

An unanswered Chaput may move to the archive after 24 hours. Eligible archived conversations can later be brought back with Chaput's Revive feature, so the conversation does not need to disappear forever.

Are Chaput profiles private?

Yes. New Chaput accounts are private by default. A follow request must be approved before a new connection can interact in the ways permitted by the app, and conversation visibility still depends on the relevant profile settings.

What safety tools does Chaput provide?

Chaput includes reporting, blocking, restricting, private profiles, follow approval and limits designed to reduce repeated unsolicited contact. Users should report content or behavior that violates the platform's rules and use blocking or restricting when needed.

What is the minimum age for Chaput?

Chaput is intended for users aged 15 and older. Users who do not meet the applicable minimum age requirement may not create an account or continue using the service.

Is Chaput available on iPhone and Android?

Yes. Chaput is available for supported iOS and Android devices. Use the official App Store and Google Play buttons on this page to view current availability in your region.

How do I download Chaput?

Use the official App Store or Google Play link on this page. Chaput should be downloaded from the official stores rather than unofficial package or APK websites.

How can I report a user or conversation?

Use the reporting controls available in Chaput to report a user, message or conversation. You can also block or restrict an account when appropriate, and the official support contact is listed in the footer and legal pages.

What does "Conversations become content" mean?

It means a conversation can be more than a private exchange. Subject to profile privacy and visibility rules, Chaput allows appropriate conversations to be discovered and read as part of a profile's content.
