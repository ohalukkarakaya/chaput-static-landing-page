A calmer first step

A first message should feel simple.

Meeting someone new does not have to begin with a perfect line or an instant conversation. Chaput gives you time to explore the profile, find something genuine and leave one message that the other person can answer when they choose.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

You do not need to impress everyone

Look for one person whose profile genuinely interests you. A specific, honest question creates a stronger opening than trying to sound universally clever.

The pace belongs to both people

You choose when to send the first Chaput; the recipient chooses whether and when to reply. This shared pace makes room for interest without turning attention into pressure.

Comfort includes the ability to stop

Private profiles, follow approval, reporting, restricting and blocking controls help people manage who can reach them and how visible their profile becomes.

Frequently asked questions

How does Chaput reduce first-message pressure?

You begin with profile context and one message, then give the recipient space to decide whether to reply.

Do I need a clever opening line?

No. A sincere question about something in the profile is usually a better start.

Can I control who interacts with me?

Yes. Privacy, follow, reporting, restricting and blocking tools support that control.
