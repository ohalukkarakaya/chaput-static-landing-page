How Chaput works

Find a profile that interests you, send a follow request when approval is required, and leave one opening Chaput. The conversation begins only if the other person replies.

Download ChaputWhat is Chaput?

1. Find a profile that interests you

You can browse suggested profiles or search by name or username. A profile may include interests, personal details and visible conversations that help you understand the person before you contact them.

Profiles are private by default. In many cases, the person needs to approve your follow request before you can send a Chaput.

2. Send one opening Chaput

Once you can contact the person, you send one opening message. It can be a question, a reaction to something on their profile or anything else that gives them a clear reason to reply.

You cannot keep sending more messages while you wait. This gives the other person space to decide whether they want to continue.

3. The conversation starts with a reply

If the recipient replies within 24 hours, the Chaput becomes a one to one conversation. Depending on the visibility settings, the conversation may also appear on the profile tree.

Whisper can be used for private messages inside an eligible conversation. Those messages are visible only to the participants.

4. Archive and Revive

If there is no reply within 24 hours, the unanswered Chaput may move to Archive. It will no longer remain active in the same way as an ongoing conversation.

Some archived conversations can later be reopened with Revive. This gives the sender another chance without turning the first message into repeated contact.

5. Privacy and safety controls

You can manage follow relationships, change profile and conversation visibility, report content, and block or restrict accounts. These controls decide who can contact you and who can see your interactions.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to follow someone before sending a Chaput?

Profiles are private by default, so a follow request may need to be approved before you can send a Chaput.

How many opening messages can I send?

You can send one opening Chaput and must wait for a reply before the conversation continues.

What happens after 24 hours?

If there is no reply within 24 hours, the Chaput may move to Archive. Eligible archived conversations can later be reopened with Revive.

What is Whisper?

Whisper lets eligible participants send a private message inside a conversation.
