How it works

From a profile to a real conversation.

Discover a profile, connect when approval is needed and leave one opening Chaput. The other person can then decide whether to continue the conversation.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

1. Discover someone you want to know

Browse suggested profiles or search by name or username. Because profiles are private by default, you may need an approved follow request before you can send a Chaput.

2. Write one thoughtful opener

Choose something genuine from the profile and use it to begin. You can send one opening Chaput, so the first contact stays clear and the recipient is never faced with a stream of follow-up messages.

3. A reply turns it into a chat

If the recipient replies within 24 hours, the Chaput becomes a conversation. When visibility settings allow, that conversation can remain connected to the profile tree. Whisper messages stay between eligible participants.

If no reply arrives, the Chaput may move to the archive. Eligible archived conversations can later be reopened with Revive.

You stay in control

You can manage follow relationships, report content, and block or restrict accounts. Profile and conversation settings determine who can see each interaction.

Frequently asked questions

How many opening messages can I send?

You can leave one opening Chaput and must wait for a reply before the conversation continues.

What happens after 24 hours?

An unanswered Chaput may move to the archive. Eligible conversations can later be reopened with Revive.

What is Whisper?

Whisper is a private message layer available only to eligible conversation participants.
