Conversation discovery

Discover people through the conversations that shape their profiles.

A profile can tell you more than a photo and a short bio. On Chaput, conversations become part of the profile tree when privacy settings allow, giving you a richer sense of the personality, humour and ideas behind the profile.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

See more than a polished introduction

A conversation can reveal timing, warmth, curiosity and the small details that make someone memorable. The profile tree keeps those moments connected to the person and their chosen privacy settings.

Find a natural reason to say hello

When you notice a shared interest or a conversation that stays with you, the first message no longer has to come from nowhere. You can begin with one thoughtful Chaput that fits the profile you just explored.

Discovery remains in each person's control

Profiles begin private, follow requests may require approval and people can report, restrict or block unwanted interactions. What you can see depends on the relationship and the profile owner's settings.

Frequently asked questions

What does conversation discovery mean?

It means learning about people through conversations attached to their profile tree, where visibility and privacy rules allow.

Can everyone see every conversation?

No. Visibility depends on profile privacy, the people involved and the relevant relationship settings.

How do I start a conversation?

Open a profile, choose a genuine point of connection and leave one Chaput. A reply opens the conversation.
