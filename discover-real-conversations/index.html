Conversation discovery

Discover people through the conversations around them.

Chaput is a social app where a conversation can remain part of a profile tree when its visibility settings allow. Instead of reducing people to isolated posts, Chaput gives memorable exchanges a place to keep their context.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

A conversation says more than a caption

Timing, humour, curiosity and the way someone listens can reveal the character of an exchange. Chaput keeps eligible conversations connected to the profile where they began, helping discovery feel human rather than interchangeable.

Visible never means ownerless

Some conversations are visible only when profile and Chaput rules permit it. Whisper messages remain participant-only. A visible exchange is still connected to real people and should not be copied, republished or commercially used without appropriate permission.

From discovery to your own first move

When a profile catches your attention, begin with something real from it. One opening Chaput gives the other person room to answer; if they reply within the available window, the exchange becomes a conversation of its own.

Frequently asked questions

Can everyone see every conversation?

No. Visibility depends on the relevant profile and Chaput rules, and Whisper messages are participant-only.

Can I reuse a visible conversation as content?

Visibility is not automatic permission to reproduce or commercialise a conversation. Respect privacy, consent and platform rules.

How are conversations connected to profiles?

Eligible conversations can remain attached to the profile tree where they began, preserving useful context.
