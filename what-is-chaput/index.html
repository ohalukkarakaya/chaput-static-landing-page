Product overview

What is Chaput?

Chaput is a conversation-first social app where people discover profiles, connect through approved follow relationships and start with one intentional first Chaput.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

A profile can hold more than a bio

On Chaput, a profile is also a place where conversations can grow. When visibility and privacy rules allow, chats remain connected to the profile tree that gave them context. That makes discovery feel less like browsing isolated posts and more like meeting people through the conversations around them.

Profiles are private by default. Some first interactions require an approved follow request before a Chaput can be left.

One first message, then room for a reply

A sender can leave one opening Chaput. Until the receiver replies, the sender cannot stack more messages on top of it. If the receiver answers within 24 hours, the Chaput becomes a conversation. Unanswered Chaputs may move to the archive, and eligible conversations can later use Revive.

Whisper messages add a participant-only layer inside an eligible conversation.

Who Chaput is for

Chaput is for people who prefer profile-led discovery, a calmer first move and conversations with context. It can also give dialogue, commentary and storytelling creators ideas about formats and conversation dynamics when a conversation is visible.

Chaput is not a roulette-style anonymous chat service, a traditional dating app or a promise of a match.

Frequently asked questions

Is Chaput a dating app?

No. Chaput is a social app for discovering profiles and starting conversations. It does not promise romantic matches or relationships.

Are Chaput profiles public?

Profiles are private by default. Visibility depends on profile, follow and conversation rules.

Can people send repeated first messages?

No. A sender leaves one initial Chaput and waits for the receiver to reply.
