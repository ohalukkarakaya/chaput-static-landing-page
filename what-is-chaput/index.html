Meet Chaput

What is Chaput?

Chaput is a social discovery app where people are discovered through profiles, interests and visible conversations—not random matching. You start with one thoughtful opening message and wait for a reply.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

Discover people with context

A Chaput profile brings together a person, their interests and the conversations connected to their profile tree. When visibility settings allow, those conversations show how someone thinks, responds and expresses themselves—giving you more context than a photo and a short bio alone.

Profiles are private by default. A follow request may need to be approved before you can leave a Chaput, so discovery happens through intentional connections rather than random matching.

Start with one thoughtful message

You leave one opening Chaput and wait for the other person to respond. If they reply within 24 hours, that opening becomes a one-to-one conversation. Until they respond, you cannot keep sending more messages.

If there is no reply, the Chaput can move to Archive. Eligible archived conversations may later be reopened with Revive. Whisper gives participants a private space inside an eligible conversation.

Conversations can become part of a profile

When visibility settings allow, a conversation can be read as part of the profile it belongs to. This turns dialogue into context: people can discover personalities, viewpoints, humour and communication styles through real exchanges.

Visible conversations can also inspire writers, commentary creators and dialogue-focused creators looking for interesting formats or themes.

What Chaput is, and what it is not

Chaput is a profile-based social discovery app for thoughtful conversations. It is not a random chat service, anonymous roulette or a promise of romantic matching.

The app is designed around private-by-default profiles, follow approval, reporting, blocking and conversation-level visibility controls so people can connect with more context and less pressure.

Frequently asked questions

What kind of app is Chaput?

Chaput is a social discovery app built around profiles, visible conversations and one thoughtful opening message. It helps people learn more about someone before deciding to start a conversation.

Is Chaput a dating app?

No. Chaput is designed for discovering people and starting conversations. It does not promise romantic matches or relationships.

Is Chaput a random or anonymous chat app?

No. Chaput does not randomly match strangers. Conversations begin from a profile, and access depends on privacy settings, follow relationships and approval.

Are profiles public?

Profiles are private by default. What you can see depends on profile settings, follow relationships and conversation visibility.

Can I send several opening messages?

No. You leave one opening Chaput and wait for the other person to reply.
