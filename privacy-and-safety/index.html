Privacy and safety

Simple controls for safer, more comfortable conversations.

Chaput combines private profiles with clear follow and safety controls, so you can decide who gets closer and act quickly when an interaction becomes unwanted.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

Private from the beginning

New profiles start private. Follow approval and relationship-based visibility help keep discovery aligned with the choices of the people involved.

Tools you can use immediately

Report content that breaks the rules, restrict an account when you want less interaction or block someone to stop direct contact. Published community and child-safety standards explain what Chaput does not allow.

Everyday safety still matters

Protect personal information, be cautious with off-platform contact and use a public place if you decide to meet someone. If you face immediate danger, contact the appropriate local authorities.

Frequently asked questions

How do I report a problem?

Use the in-app report option on the relevant profile, conversation or message.

What is the difference between restricting and blocking?

Restricting limits interaction, while blocking is intended to stop direct contact and visibility between the accounts.

Where can I read Chaput's safety rules?

The public legal and safety pages are linked from Chaput's website and in-app flows where relevant.
