Privacy and safety

Clear choices for who gets closer.

Chaput combines private profiles by default with follow approval where required, layered conversation visibility and in-app reporting, blocking and restricting. These features support boundaries; they do not replace personal caution or guarantee safety.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

Know what can be visible

A profile begins private. Some Chaput conversations can remain visible when the relevant profile and conversation settings allow, while Whisper messages are participant-only. Check the context before sharing anything personal or sensitive.

Use boundaries early

Declining a follow request, not replying, blocking or restricting are valid choices. Repeated unwanted contact, harassment, threats, sexualisation and attempts to pressure someone off-platform should be reported.

Help is documented and reachable

Use in-app reporting for accounts, profiles, messages or content. Chaput’s published Community Guidelines, Content Moderation Policy, Privacy Policy and Children’s Privacy Notice explain the applicable standards. For immediate danger, contact local emergency services.

Frequently asked questions

Does Chaput guarantee user safety?

No social platform can eliminate all risk. Chaput provides privacy and moderation controls that should be used alongside ordinary online-safety practices.

What can I report?

The app supports reporting of relevant users, profiles, messages or content through the available in-app controls.

Where can I read Chaput’s safety rules?

The Legal center links to the Community Guidelines, Moderation Policy, Privacy Policy and Children’s Privacy Notice.
