Interest is fine; reduction is not

Being attracted to goth style is not inherently wrong. A respectful connection begins by treating the person as an individual, not as an aesthetic category, fantasy or collectible. Read what she chose to share before deciding what to say.

Better places and better openings

Alternative music events, art spaces, fashion-making communities and scene-specific online groups offer genuine context. Ask about a shared interest. “I have always wanted a goth girlfriend” is a poor opening because it assigns a role before knowing the person.

Respect the answer, including silence

A declined follow request, no reply or a direct no ends the approach. Do not repeat messages, request photos, sexualise the person or push for off-platform contact. Chaput users can report, block and restrict inappropriate behaviour.

What Chaput does and does not promise

Chaput’s follow approval and one-message-until-reply flow can support clearer boundaries. Chaput is not a dating app and does not promise a girlfriend, match or relationship. It is for users aged 15 and older, subject to higher local age rules.
