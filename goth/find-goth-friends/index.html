Friendship guide

Let a shared interest make the first message easier.

Friendship grows more naturally when the conversation begins with something real. A favourite band, event, book, game or creative project can give both people an easy place to start.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

Look beyond the profile photo

Read the details people choose to share. Style may catch your attention, but interests and personality give the conversation somewhere to go.

Ask a question with an easy answer

Mention one specific detail and ask about it without testing the other person's knowledge. A warm, focused question feels more inviting than a list of compliments.

Give friendship time to develop

Not every request will be accepted and not every conversation will become a friendship. Respect each person's pace, avoid repeated messages and let mutual interest guide what happens next.

Frequently asked questions

Where can I find goth friends online?

Try music and art communities, alternative-interest groups and profile-led social apps where people share some context first.

What should I say in the first message?

Choose one genuine detail from the profile and ask a simple, thoughtful question about it.

Is Chaput a dating app?

Chaput is a social app for discovering people and starting conversations; it does not promise a date or relationship.
