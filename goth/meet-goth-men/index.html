Respectful connection guide

Start with the person, not the stereotype.

Shared music, art, games, events and creative interests can offer a genuine reason to talk. Read the profile, ask about something specific and let the person define himself through his own words.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

Use shared culture as a real opening

Ask about a band, instrument, event, artwork, game or project that appears on the profile. A considered question shows interest without assigning a personality based on style.

Do not cast someone in a role

Goth style does not mean a person will be distant, mysterious, dominant or romantically interested. Meet the individual in front of you instead of expecting a character.

Respect boundaries in every direction

Do not repeat messages, pressure for photos or demand contact elsewhere. A declined request or no reply is enough; let the conversation end there.

Frequently asked questions

Where can I meet goth men?

Alternative events, music and art communities, interest groups and profile-led social apps can provide useful context.

How should I start the conversation?

Read the profile, mention a genuine shared interest and ask one respectful question.

Is Chaput a dating service?

No. Chaput is a social app for discovering people and starting conversations; it does not guarantee romantic outcomes.
