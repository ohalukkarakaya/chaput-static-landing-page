Goth and alternative community

Find your place in the goth community.

Chaput is a profile-first social app where goth and alternative people can discover one another, connect through approved follow relationships and begin real conversations. Chaput has zero tolerance for harassment, unwanted sexualisation and treating people as stereotypes or fantasies.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

Be seen as a person, not an aesthetic

Music, art, fashion and subculture can create a real point of connection. They should not reduce someone to a “goth fantasy,” collectible look or assumed personality. Read the profile and talk to the individual in front of you.

Find people around shared interests

Chaput is designed to provide a space where people of all kinds, including goth and alternative users, can find one another. Profile discovery can make a specific band, event, creative practice or local scene a more natural first subject.

Boundaries are part of the culture

Accept a declined request or unanswered message. Do not pressure anyone for photos, sexual conversation or off-platform contact. Users can report, block and restrict when needed, and the one-message-until-reply rule limits repeated first contact.

Goth, emo, punk and metal are not interchangeable labels

People can participate in more than one scene, but each culture has its own histories, music and meanings. Ask rather than assign a label. Curiosity is welcome; gatekeeping and stereotyping are not.

Frequently asked questions

Is Chaput only for goth users?

No. Chaput is for many kinds of people and interests, including goth and alternative users.

Is Chaput a goth dating app?

No. It is a social app for profile discovery and conversation, not a dating or matchmaking service.

How should I approach a goth person?

Read their profile, begin with a genuine shared interest and avoid sexualised or stereotype-based assumptions. Respect no response or rejection.
