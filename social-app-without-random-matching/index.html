A different way to discover

Choose the profile before you choose the words.

Chaput offers profile-first social discovery without anonymous roulette-style matching. You see a person’s profile and context first, connect when approval is required, and decide whether there is a genuine reason to begin a conversation.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

Context before contact

A profile gives you something concrete to respond to: music, art, a project, a place or a point of view. This makes the first message less like a performance for a stranger and more like the natural next step after noticing something specific.

Connection is not a guaranteed match

Chaput is not a dating or matchmaking service and does not promise friendship, romance or a reply. It creates a clearer route from discovering a profile to leaving one opening message, while the other person keeps the choice to respond.

When this approach fits

Profile-first discovery may suit people who want more context, fewer repeated opening messages and more control over who reaches the next step. People who prefer instant anonymous pairings may find other services better suited to that format.

Frequently asked questions

Does Chaput randomly pair strangers?

No. Chaput uses profile-first discovery rather than anonymous roulette-style matching.

Is Chaput a matchmaking app?

No. Chaput supports social discovery and conversation without promising a match, relationship or response.

Do I need follow approval?

Profiles are private by default, so some interactions require an approved follow relationship.
