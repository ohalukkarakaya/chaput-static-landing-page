Profile-first discovery

Discover the profile before you start the conversation.

Chaput gives the first message somewhere to come from. Explore a person's profile tree, notice a genuine point of connection and begin with one Chaput rather than an instant stream of messages.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

Context makes the opening easier

A shared interest, a detail in the profile or a visible conversation can offer a natural first question. You do not have to invent a performance for someone you know nothing about.

One message creates a calmer start

The first Chaput gives the other person room to decide whether they want to continue. When they reply, the exchange becomes an open conversation.

Privacy stays part of the experience

Profiles start private and follow approval can shape what becomes visible. Reporting, restricting and blocking tools are available when an interaction is unwanted.

Frequently asked questions

How is Chaput different from instant matching?

Chaput begins with profile context and one first message instead of placing two strangers directly into a live conversation.

Are profiles private?

New profiles start private, and visibility can change according to follow relationships and profile settings.

What happens after I send a Chaput?

The recipient can reply if they want to continue. That reply opens the conversation.
