Privacy by default

Your profile starts private. You decide who gets closer.

A new Chaput profile begins private, so visibility grows through the choices you make rather than being assumed from the start. Follow requests, profile settings and safety tools help you shape the space around you.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

Choose who enters your circle

A follow request gives you a chance to review the person before approving access. What someone can see depends on the relationship, the conversations involved and your current settings.

Keep conversations connected to their context

When a conversation is visible, it remains part of the profile tree instead of becoming a detached post. Privacy rules still determine who can see it.

Change course when you need to

You can remove followers and use reporting, restricting or blocking controls when an interaction no longer feels right. Privacy is an ongoing choice, not a one-time screen.

Frequently asked questions

Are new Chaput profiles private?

Yes. New profiles start private.

Can I choose who follows me?

Private profiles can review and approve follow requests before access is granted.

Can I block or restrict another user?

Yes. Chaput includes reporting, restricting and blocking controls for unwanted interactions.
