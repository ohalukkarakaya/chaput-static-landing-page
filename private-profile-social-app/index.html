Privacy by default

Your profile begins private. Connection begins with a choice.

Chaput profiles are private by default. Follow approval and conversation visibility rules determine what becomes available next, so discovery can happen without treating every profile and conversation as public by default.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

Private does not mean invisible forever

People can still be discovered through the product’s available search and suggestion surfaces, but access to the next interaction may require an approved follow. The exact view depends on profile, relationship and Chaput rules.

Conversation privacy has layers

Eligible conversations may be visible on a profile tree when the applicable settings permit it. Whisper messages remain available only to eligible participants. Users should still avoid sharing sensitive personal information in any social app.

Controls for when something feels wrong

Chaput includes tools to report content or accounts and to block or restrict users. These controls support user choice but cannot remove every online risk, so ordinary digital safety habits remain important.

Frequently asked questions

Are Chaput profiles private by default?

Yes. New profiles begin private, and some interactions require follow approval.

Are all Chaput conversations private?

Not necessarily. Visibility depends on profile and conversation rules; Whisper messages are participant-only.

Can I block or restrict another user?

Yes. Chaput provides blocking, restricting and reporting controls in the app.
