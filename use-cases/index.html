Ways to use Chaput

A profile can be the beginning of a great conversation.

Chaput gives you a more thoughtful way to meet people: discover the profile first, find a genuine point of interest and begin with one message.

Download ChaputLearn how it works

Take the pressure out of the first message

A good opener does not need to impress. Read the profile, pick a real point of connection and leave one Chaput. The other person can respond in their own time without receiving repeated follow-ups.

A simple way to begin
Mention a shared interest, ask a specific question and leave assumptions out of the conversation.

Turn conversation patterns into original ideas

A visible exchange can spark ideas for a meme, voice-over, commentary piece, scene or podcast topic. Look for the timing, misunderstanding or unexpected reply that makes the exchange memorable, then create something in your own voice.

Respect privacy, consent and the people behind every conversation. Visibility is not automatic permission to republish someone else’s words or identity.

Connect through shared interests

Music, art, games, local culture and alternative scenes can all offer an easy place to begin. Chaput lets you see the person and their interests before you decide what to say.

Frequently asked questions

Can creators use Chaput for inspiration?

Yes. Visible conversation formats can inspire original work, provided privacy, consent and platform rules are respected.

Is Chaput only for creators?

No. Chaput is first and foremost a social app for discovering people and starting conversations.

Can I find people with similar interests?

Yes. Profiles and suggestions can help you discover people whose interests give you a natural starting point.
